6 #form-styles Form styles

For fancy form styling

6.1 #form-styles.combo Combo

On medium and up, displays input and button right next to each other. On small screens they're stacked.

Example 
<div class="slab slab--primary">
  <div class="container">
  <div class="combo">
    <input type="text" class="combo__input">
    <button class="button--primary combo__button">Go</button>
  </div>
</div>
</div>
